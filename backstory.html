What happened to Bruce Wayne?

As a child, Bruce Wayne witnesses the murder of his parents, Thomas and Martha Wayne, in a mugging gone wrong.
This traumatic event drives him to dedicate his life to fighting crime and protecting Gotham City from evildoers.
To do so, he adopts the persona of Batman, a vigilante who uses his intellect, martial arts skills, detective abilities, and advanced technology to combat crime.
